Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I limit Mac battery charging to 80%?

Lithium-ion batteries experience the most chemical stress when kept at 100% or exposed to high heat. VeryBattery helps extend lifespan by keeping it at the optimal 80% level.

What is VeryBattery's Sailing mode?

Instead of staying static at a fixed percentage, Sailing mode allows the battery to discharge and recharge slightly, maintaining cell activity even while plugged in.

How does stay_cool work?

Charging generates heat. During heavy tasks, this combined heat can damage the battery. VeryBattery monitors temperature and pauses charging when it gets too hot.
